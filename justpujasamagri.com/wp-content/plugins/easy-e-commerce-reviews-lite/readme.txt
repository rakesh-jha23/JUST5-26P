The best and easiest way to add reviews to your e-commerce products in WordPress.

== Description ==

**This is the LITE version of Easy E-commerce Reviews. The pro version is packed with advanced features like: schema.org support for better SEO, required ratings, verified customer badge, review voting, require reviewers to be verified customers, etc! Check it out! <a href="http://cgd.io/easy-e-commerce-reviews/">Easy E-commerce Reviews PRO</a>**

Product reviews are one of the most important things you can add to your e-commerce site.  Product reviews and ratings reduce buyer anxiety and increase sales…even if some of the reviews are negative.  It’s win win!  Easy E-commerce Reviews will add reviews to your cart in minutes.

We support WooCommerce, Shopp, iThemes Exchange, Easy Digital Downloads, Jigoshop, and WP eCommerce out of the box!

And because it leverages the native comment system, it is completely compatible with WordPress.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the directory “easy-ecommerce-reviews-lite“ to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Find “Easy E-commerce Reviews Lite“ in menu on left for documentation and settings.
